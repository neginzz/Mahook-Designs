I'm Ahmad Mahook, a UI/UX Designer with a Law degree and a curious heart.

I didn't start in design. I studied Law, but deep down, I always wanted to create. So in my final year of college, I made a bold shift and started a career in design; and I've never looked back.

Design gave me a place to blend everything I've collected in my life: Logic and structure from Law, imagination from cinema and music, and a lifelong curiosity about people and how they think

What drives me? People! I love getting to know them, really listening not just to what they say, but how they say it. I'm always curious about how the human mind works, how people feel, and why they do what they do, and I ask questions a lot.
